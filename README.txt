Get low conversions with your site?

= Installation Instructions =

1. Search "Cometleads" on your wordpress admin portal in the *Plugin* Menu
2. Download and activate the plugin called 'Cometleads Contact'
3. Once you activated the plugin, you will notice a *Settings" link next to it

To uninstall, go to your plugin page, find 'Cometleads Contact'. Click 'deactivate' and then 'Delete'.
